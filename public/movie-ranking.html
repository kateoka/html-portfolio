The Best Movies

My top 3 movies of all time.

The Shawshank Redemption

It’s a deeply human story about hope, resilience, and friendship, set against the grim backdrop of prison life.

The Godfather

Its storytelling, direction, and cinematography set a new benchmark for film.

The Dark Knight

It transformed the superhero film into something more—blending action, philosophy, and moral ambiguity.
